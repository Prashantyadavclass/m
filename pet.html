UPSSSC Preliminary Examination Test (PET) 2025

Posted On: 02 May 2025 | 06:14 PM

Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the official notification for Preliminary Examination Test (PET) 2025 (Advertisement No. 01-Exam/2025). This examination serves as the first stage screening test for various Group B and Group C posts under the Uttar Pradesh government. Candidates who qualify PET will be eligible to apply for subsequent recruitment processes. Online applications can be submitted from 14 May 2025 to 17 June 2025.

UPSSSC PET Exam 2025 Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Candidate must have passed Intermediate (10+2) examination or equivalent from any recognized board in India
  • Higher qualification (Graduate/Post Graduate) is acceptable
  • Technical/professional qualifications recognized by the government are also valid
  • Final year appearing candidates can also apply

Nationality

  • Must be a citizen of India
  • Nepalese/Bhutanese subjects may apply as per Government of India rules
  • Tibetan refugees who came to India before 1st January 1962
  • Persons of Indian origin migrated from specified countries

Domicile

  • Candidate must be a domicile of Uttar Pradesh
  • Non-domicile candidates can apply but may have different reservation benefits
  • Domicile certificate required for reservation benefits
Exam Pattern & Syllabus

Exam Duration

2 Hours (120 Minutes) for General Candidates | 2 Hours 40 Minutes (160 Minutes) for PwD Candidates

Question Type

Objective Type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Total Questions

100 Questions (Each carrying 1 mark)

Negative Marking

0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer

Syllabus Breakdown

General Knowledge (40 Marks)

  • Current events of national and international importance
  • Indian History and National Movement
  • Indian and World Geography
  • Indian Polity and Governance
  • Economic and Social Development
  • Environmental Ecology, Biodiversity and Climate Change
  • General Science and Technology
  • Uttar Pradesh specific knowledge

General Intelligence & Reasoning (30 Marks)

  • Analogies, similarities and differences
  • Space visualization, problem solving, analysis
  • Decision making, visual memory, discrimination
  • Observation, relationship concepts
  • Arithmetical reasoning, verbal and figure classification
  • Arithmetical number series, coding-decoding
  • Statement conclusion, syllogistic reasoning

Hindi Language (15 Marks)

  • Vocabulary
  • Grammar
  • Sentence structure
  • Synonyms and antonyms
  • Comprehension

Basic Numerical Ability (15 Marks)

  • Number systems
  • Fundamental arithmetical operations
  • Percentages, ratios and proportions
  • Averages, interest, profit and loss
  • Discount, use of tables and graphs
Selection Process
Stage 1

Preliminary Examination Test (PET)

Screening test to shortlist candidates for various posts. Score remains valid for 1 year from result declaration.

Stage 2

Mains Examination (Post Specific)

Only PET qualified candidates can apply. Different for each recruitment based on post requirements.

Stage 3

Document Verification

Verification of original documents, certificates, and eligibility criteria.

Stage 4

Medical Examination (If applicable)

For certain posts requiring physical/medical standards.

PET Score Benefits

Eligibility

  • PET score makes candidates eligible to apply for various UPSSSC recruitments
  • Different posts may have different cutoff requirements
  • Score remains valid for 1 year from result declaration

Cutoff Determination

  • Category-wise cutoff marks
  • Based on difficulty level and number of vacancies
  • Previous year cutoff trends available on official website

Score Utilization

  • Single PET score can be used for multiple recruitments
  • No need to reappear for PET during validity period
  • Higher score increases chances for multiple posts
How to Apply Online
  1. Visit Official Website - Go to UPSSSC's official website (upsssc.gov.in)
  2. Registration - Click on "New Registration" and fill required details
  3. Login - Use registration number and password to login
  4. Fill Application Form - Enter personal, educational and contact details carefully
  5. Upload Documents - Upload scanned photograph, signature and required certificates
  6. Fee Payment - Pay application fee through available online modes
  7. Submit Application - Review all details and submit the form
  8. Print Application - Take printout of submitted application for future reference

Important Notes

  • Keep scanned copies of photograph (20-50kb) and signature (10-20kb) ready before applying
  • Photograph should be recent passport size with white background
  • Signature should be done on white paper with black ink
  • All documents should be in specified format and size
  • Double check all entered information before final submission
  • No changes allowed after final submission except during correction window
