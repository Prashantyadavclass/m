UPSSSC PET Exam 2025 Eligibility

Exam Name: Pre Examination Test (UPSSSC PET 2025)

Educational Qualification: Minimum Class 10 High School Exam from Any Recognized Board OR Any Higher Qualification from Any Recognized University in India.

More About UPSSSC PET Form 2025:
Uttar Pradesh UPSSSC has released the advertisement for preliminary examination. Those who are eligible for high school or more, then register for this examination immediately. In future, all types of government jobs that will be conducted by UPSSSC, it will be necessary to pass this exam.
How to Fill UPSSSC PET 2025 Online Form
  1. Candidate Can Apply Between 14/05/2025 to 17/06/2025.
  2. Read the Notification Before Apply.
  3. Check and Collect All Documents - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
  4. Have Scanned Documents Ready - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, etc.
  5. Check Preview and All Columns Carefully Before Final Submission.
  6. Take a Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
